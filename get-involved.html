About Us

Chester Garden Hub is a testament to the transformative power of nature in building and strengthening communities. Born from the passion and dedication of a group of local individuals during the lockdown of 2020, our vision was to create a vibrant, sustainable garden space that would welcome and unite people of all ages and backgrounds.

Through our collective efforts, wewere able to gain the support of local authority businesses and private donors, enabling our dream to become a reality. By the late summer of 2021, Chester Garden Hub proudly opened its gates. Nestled near the serene Countess Park in Chester, our garden provides a space for everyone to come, cultivate, learn, and connect.

Our Mission

Our mission is to foster a sense of belonging and promote sustainable living practices through the establishment of a community garden. By providing a shared space for gardening, education, and community engagement, we aim to empower individuals and strengthen the fabric of our neighborhood.
