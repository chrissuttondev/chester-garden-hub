Volunteer

Volunteer Opportunities: We have a number volunteer opportunities avaialble. Whether it's maintaining the garden, organizing events, or leading workshops, volunteering is a wonderful way to contribute to the success of our community garden project and make a difference in the lives of others. If you are interested in volunteering with us then we'd love to hear you.

Membership

Are you passionate about gardening, sustainability, and community engagement? We invite you to join us in our mission to create a thriving community garden. We have a number of plots available to let at affordable rates. By becoming a member, you'll have access to a dedicated gardening plot, educational resources, and the opportunity to connect with like-minded individuals. Together, we can cultivate not only beautiful gardens but also a stronger, greener community.
